What You Get

Built for real student
collaboration

Every feature designed around how students actually work and connect.

🎯

Skill Match Percentage

Our algorithm compares your skills against potential partners and project needs, giving you a compatibility score so you know instantly who's a great fit.

💡

Idea Pitch Cards

Post your project as a structured pitch with title, description, tech stack, difficulty level, and how many teammates you need. Clear, scannable, and actionable.

🔄

Collaboration Mode Toggle

Switch between "Looking for Partners" (you have an idea) and "Ready to Join" (you want to contribute) modes. Your visibility adapts instantly.

🧩

Interest-Based Discovery

Browse by interest areas: Web Dev, AI/ML, App Dev, Open Source, and Hackathons. Find projects and people aligned with what excites you most.

📊

Project Readiness Score

A dynamic score based on your profile completeness, recent activity, and project details. Higher score = more visibility and better matches.

🌍

Nearby Collaborators

Optionally surface students from the same college or city for easier in-person collaboration. IRL teamwork, enabled digitally.

🔗

Quick Connect Actions

Invite, Collaborate, or Save partners with a single click. No friction, no lengthy forms — just direct action when inspiration strikes.

Beginner-Friendly
